The gridded surfaces (*.tif) contained in the Majority.tar.gz archive represent the majority land use contained within grid cells of 250x250m, for each year from 1940-2020.


Historical Land Use for the U.S. 1940-2015
Abbreviations and explanations:
Geo-Referenced:		Records are georeferenced if an explicit latitude and longitude is provided in ZTRAX, Building records from Open City Model and Parcel Atlas used the centroid points of the polygons.
Missing Grid-ID:	Records without an explicit latitude and longitude, which could not be grid-indexed.
Grid Cell Value:	The most frequent land use per grid cell using one of eight land use themes
		'Theme1': 		Agriculture
		'Theme2': 		Commercial
		'Theme3': 		Industrial
		'Theme4': 		Recreational
		'Theme5': 		Residential-Income
		'Theme6': 		Residential-Owned
		'Theme7': 		Governmental
		'Theme8': 		VacantLand
